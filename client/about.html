Our Story

BananaPrep was born from a simple observation: while programming interview prep resources are abundant, there's a gap in practice platforms for electrical engineering students. We founded BananaPrep to bridge this gap by providing a comprehensive platform for students and professionals to practice, learn, and master engineering concepts.

Our platform offers a wide array of engineering practice problems to ensure your best performance in interviews and in the workforce. By focusing on industry-relevant problems and providing interactive solutions, we help students develop the critical thinking and analytical skills needed in today's competitive technical landscape.

Our Mission

To create an accessible, comprehensive platform that empowers electrical engineers to hone their problem-solving skills through practice, collaboration, and expert guidance.

Our Vision

A world where every engineering student has access to quality practice resources, enabling them to build confidence, excel in their academics, and become innovative problem solvers in their careers.

Meet Our Team

BananaPrep is powered by a passionate team of electrical engineers, educators, and software developers committed to creating the best learning experience for our users.
