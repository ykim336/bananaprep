Ohm's Law Calculation Easy
Find the current through the circuit using Ohm's law. A 10V battery is connected to a 5Ω resistor. What is the current in amperes?
Examples
Input:
V = 10, R = 5
Output:
2
Explanation:
According to Ohm's Law: I = V/R = 10/5 = 2 amperes
Constraints
  • Voltage (V) is between 1 and 100
  • Resistance (R) is between 1 and 50
